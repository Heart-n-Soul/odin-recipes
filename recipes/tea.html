Tea

Description

This recipe for preparing tea is predominant among West-Africans but it is enjoyed by people all over the world, because it is not only appetizing, but also very easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • milo
  • sugar
  • milk (Diary or non diary)
  • water
  • bread
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. pour your milo or chocolate powder into any cup of your choice according to your desired quantity.
  2. add your sugar
  3. add your powdered milk
  4. pour water into the mixture and stir till adequately mixed
  5. add your liquid milk and or ice cubes if you prefer those.
  6. stir again and it is ready.
  7. you can serve with bread
