Tombrown

Description

This recipe for preparing tombrown is predominant among West-Africans but it is enjoyed by people all over the world, because it is not only appetizing, but also very easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • tombrown powder, made of grinded fried maize, groundnut, soya beans, etc.
  • sugar
  • milk (Diary or non diary)
  • water

Steps

  1. pour your powdered tombrown into a bowl and add water.
  2. mix until even
  3. pour mixture into a cooking bowl and bring it to boil
  4. you can make it as thick as you want or add more water when it becomes thicker that you want it
  5. Ready in 5 to 15 minutes
  6. serve with sugar and milk
  7. stir until evenly mixed and serve with bread
