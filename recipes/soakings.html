Gari Soakings

Description

This recipe for preparing gari soakings is predominant among West-Africans but it is enjoyed by people all over the world, because it is not only appetizing, but also very easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • garri (cassava fried floor)
  • sugar
  • groundnut
  • milk (Diary or non diary)
  • water
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. pour your cassava fried floor into any bowl of your choice according to your desired quantity.
  2. add your sugar
  3. add your groundnut and or powdered milk
  4. pour water into the mixture and stir till adequately mixed
  5. add your liquid milk and or ice cubes if you prefer those.
  6. stir again and it is ready to eat.
